# Demo Narration

## 90-second narration

PulsePath AI is an AI care-gap intelligence platform for outpatient clinics.

One of the biggest operational problems in healthcare is that patients fall through the cracks between visits. Follow-ups, screenings, refill reviews, and discharge check-ins are often managed through manual spreadsheets and coordinator call lists.

Our product solves that by turning a patient panel into a smart outreach queue.

On the dashboard, PulsePath AI prioritises patients based on overdue days, clinical context, and revenue at risk. For each patient, the system drafts outreach for SMS, WhatsApp, and voice, but keeps a human in the loop before anything is sent.

Once outreach is approved, the care team can track patient replies, convert them into appointments, and measure recovered revenue directly inside the workflow.

We designed this as a safer and more commercial healthcare AI wedge: not diagnosis, but operational intelligence that clinics can adopt quickly and pay for with clear ROI.

PulsePath AI starts with follow-up recovery, but can expand into preventive care, chronic disease management, refill workflows, and post-discharge coordination.

## 20-second closing

PulsePath AI helps clinics close more care gaps with less manual work, while proving measurable business value through recovered appointments and stronger patient continuity.
