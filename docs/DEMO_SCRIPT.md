# PulsePath AI Demo Script

## 2-minute version

1. Open the landing page.
2. Explain the problem: clinics miss revenue and patients fall through the cracks between visits.
3. Position PulsePath AI as a care-gap intelligence layer for outpatient teams.
4. Open the dashboard.
5. Show the top insight and explain that patients are prioritized by overdue days, risk score, and revenue at risk.
6. Click a high-priority patient and show the outreach drafts for SMS, WhatsApp, and voice.
7. Emphasize the human-in-the-loop approval model.
8. Click `Approve and Send` to simulate patient engagement.
9. Click `Mark as Booked` to show booking conversion and recovered revenue.
10. End on pricing and the expansion wedge: chronic care, preventive care, discharge, and refills.

## 30-second closing line

PulsePath AI is not a generic healthcare chatbot. It is a focused operational product for clinics that turns missed follow-ups into measurable revenue recovery and better patient continuity.
