# PulsePath AI Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title

PulsePath AI

AI care-gap intelligence for outpatient clinics

Tagline: Recover missed follow-ups before they become missed outcomes.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Outpatient clinics lose patients between visits because follow-up workflows are fragmented.

- Care teams review spreadsheets, discharge notes, and call lists manually
- High-risk patients are often contacted too late
- Missed follow-ups reduce revenue and hurt continuity of care
- Coordinators spend too much time deciding who to contact and what to say

## Slide 3: Why This Matters

Every missed follow-up creates two types of loss:

- Clinical loss: delayed screening, refill review, or post-discharge check-in
- Financial loss: unused appointment slots, lower retention, and leakage to other providers

PulsePath AI turns these missed opportunities into a prioritised operational workflow.

## Slide 4: The Solution

PulsePath AI helps clinics:

- Import patient panels
- Detect overdue care gaps
- Prioritise patients by risk and revenue impact
- Generate outreach across SMS, WhatsApp, and voice
- Require staff approval before communication
- Track replies, bookings, and recovered value

## Slide 5: Product Demo Story

In the MVP:

- The dashboard surfaces the highest-priority patient first
- Staff can review outreach drafts in multiple channels
- The inbox shows simulated patient responses
- Booking a patient removes them from the queue and updates recovered revenue

This demonstrates the full workflow from data to outcome.

Buyer persona:

- Clinic administrator

- Medical director for outpatient programs

Commercial promise:
Recover several times the subscription fee through more completed follow-ups and better coordinator productivity.

## Slide 8: Why We Can Win

PulsePath AI is intentionally practical.

- Narrow workflow problem
- Easy-to-understand ROI
- Lower risk than diagnostic AI
- Human-in-the-loop by design
- Expandable across many care programs

## Slide 9: Product Flywheel

More usage creates more value.

- More clinics generate more workflow templates
- More outreach responses improve conversion patterns
- More outcome data strengthens prioritisation and benchmarking
- Better ROI drives expansion across departments and clinic groups
