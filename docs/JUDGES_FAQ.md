# Judges FAQ

## What exactly does PulsePath AI do?

PulsePath AI helps outpatient clinics detect overdue care gaps, prioritise which patients to contact first, generate outreach drafts, and track replies and bookings.

## Who pays for this product?

The most likely buyers are clinic administrators, care management teams, outpatient operations leaders, and multi-clinic practice owners.

## Why did you choose this instead of a diagnostic AI product?

This problem is easier to adopt, easier to demo, lower risk, and more commercially immediate. It solves a real healthcare workflow problem without making diagnostic claims.

## What makes the product defensible?

Over time, the system improves through workflow templates, outreach outcomes, conversion benchmarks, and operational data about which follow-up patterns drive the most value.

## Why would a clinic buy this?

Because the ROI story is straightforward: more booked follow-ups, better patient retention, more coordinator efficiency, and better visibility into revenue at risk.

## What is the MVP boundary?

The MVP supports operational workflows only. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace clinical judgment.

## How would you expand this product after the MVP?

We would expand from follow-up recovery into chronic care programs, preventive screenings, refill management, discharge workflows, and eventually a broader healthcare operations intelligence platform.

## What does success look like in a pilot?

- Faster outreach turnaround
- More completed follow-ups
- Better conversion from reminder to booking
- Clear evidence that recovered revenue exceeds subscription cost
