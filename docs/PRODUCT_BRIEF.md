# PulsePath AI Product Brief

## One-line pitch

PulsePath AI helps outpatient clinics detect overdue care gaps and convert them into booked follow-ups with AI-assisted, human-approved outreach.

## Problem

Clinic teams lose patients between visits because outreach workflows are fragmented. Coordinators manually review spreadsheets, call lists, and discharge summaries to decide who needs follow-up and what message to send.

## Solution

PulsePath AI creates one care-gap queue for care coordinators:

- Imports patient panel data
- Prioritizes overdue gaps by risk and revenue
- Generates outreach across SMS, WhatsApp, and voice
- Requires staff approval before contact
- Tracks responses and bookings

## Ideal customer profile

- Multi-specialty outpatient clinics
- Primary care groups
- Chronic care management teams
- Post-discharge coordination teams

## Why clinics would buy

- More follow-up visits booked
- Better preventive screening adherence
- Faster coordinator workflows
- Easier proof of ROI for administrators

## Pricing

- Starter: Rs 25,000/month
- Growth: Rs 75,000/month
- Enterprise: custom

## Flywheel

As clinics use PulsePath AI:

- More workflows create better templates
- More outreach outcomes improve prioritization
- More booking data strengthens ROI benchmarks
- Stronger ROI makes multi-department expansion easier

## MVP boundaries

This MVP is intentionally limited to operational workflow support. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace clinical judgment.
